# Projects

- **Spatial Decision Support System: Flood Prevention**
    <br> <br>
    `QGIS`  `SDSS`  `Weighted-Overlay`

        A QGIS-based spatial decision support system that identifies optimal green infrastructure sites in flood-prone Bushwick, NY. Identifies over 8.5 million sq ft of high-consensus impermeable land suitable for conversion to meet a hypothetical 30% neighborhood permeability requirement.

     View Project [>>](project1_SDSS.md)

- **Nova Kakhovka Dam Collapse: Impact on Rapeseed Production**
    <br> <br>
    `Google Earth Engine` `Sentinel-2` `Random Forest`

        A Random Forest classifier applied to Sentinel-2 imagery in Google Earth Engine to quantify how the 2023 dam collapse disrupted rapeseed crop area and yield in Vasylivskyi, Ukraine, finding a near-halving of crop area in 2024 followed by a faster-than-expected recovery in 2025.

    View Project[>>](project2_ukrainecrops.md)

-   **Assessing Tree Health in Central Park with NASA Earth Observation Data**
    <br><br>
    `Google Earth Engine` `ArcGIS Pro` `Landsat`

        A NASA DEVELOP feasibility study using Landsat 8 & 9 NDVI time series and logistic regression to detect Dutch Elm Disease in Central Park's American elm canopy, achieving 71% detection precision in partnership with the Central Park Conservancy.

    View Project [>>](project3_central-park.md)
